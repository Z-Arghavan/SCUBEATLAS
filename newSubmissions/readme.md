# SCUBE Atlas – Game Submission Instructions

Thank you for contributing your game to the SCUBE Atlas.

Please submit your game by adding a comment to this issue:
👉 https://github.com/Z-Arghavan/SCUBEATLAS/issues/1

## Why contribute?

By submitting your game:
- You increase the visibility of your work within the GeoGames and research community  
- Your game becomes discoverable for educators, researchers, and practitioners  
- You contribute to building a shared resource that supports learning and collaboration  
- You help advance the GeoGames field by showcasing diverse approaches and applications

## Notes
- Please keep descriptions concise.
- You can edit your comment later if needed.
- Submitted content may be shared as part of the SCUBE Atlas.

Thank you for your contribution.
